*   Problem 521

   Let smpf(n) be the smallest prime factor of n.
   smpf(91)=7 because 91=7×13 and smpf(45)=3 because 45=3×3×5.
   Let S(n) be the sum of smpf(i) for 2 ≤ i ≤ n.
   E.g. S(100)=1257.

   Find S(10^12) mod 10^9.
